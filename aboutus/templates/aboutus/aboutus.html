Our Story

We started when one of our co-founders' kid asked, 'Why there is no service like Amazon Prime for Indian Groceries?' Expanding on this question, we asked how can we use technology to eliminate mundane tasks like grocery shopping to give our customers time to do more fun things. We intend to make Bazaarprime a one-stop Online Indian Grocery store that serves all your needs.

Our Mission

We believe in providing world class service to customers shopping for Indian groceries.

Our Team

Our team is comprised of professionals with many years of experience in retail, ecommerce, logistics and technology.
