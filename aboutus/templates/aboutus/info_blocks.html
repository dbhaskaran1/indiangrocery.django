Our Story

The first spark came when one when one of our co-founders' kid asked, 'Why there is no service like Amazon Prime for Indian Groceries?' Then, we started looking at how we can use technology to facilitate mundane tasks like grocery shopping.

Our Mission

We believe in providing world class service to customers shopping for Indian groceries.

Our Team

Our team is comprised of professionals with many years of experience in retail, ecommerce, logistics and technology.

Shipping Rates

Shipping is FREE for orders over $65. For orders under $65 shipping rate is $9.99.

Returns/Exchange

We have a standard 15 day return/exchange policy. Beyond that we are unfortunately not able to offer a refund or exchange.
